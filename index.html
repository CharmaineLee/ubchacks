FAQ

What is Local Hack Day?
A local hack day is a casual event that brings students together to build cool tech-related projects over 12 hours. Itâ€™s very beginner friendly! There are no huge prizes but we do encourage sharing your projects with other fellow hackers in the end!

How much does it cost?
Nothing. Attending UBC Hacks is free!

Do I need to be a student to attend?
Yes. Unfortunately, only students who are currently enrolled at UBC, and are majoring in Computer Science can attend. (BA, BSc, BCS, BUCS) We are working on making Local Hack Day open to all students in the future.

Where is the event?
The event is being hosted at Hugh Dempster Pavilion Room 110.

Why should I come?
There will be free swag and most importantly, who doesn't want to program for 12 hours straight with fellow CS students?!
